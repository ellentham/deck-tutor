# Vintage Format Rules

Use this context when recommending cards for Vintage decks.

## Deck Structure

- **60 cards** minimum in the main deck
- **15 cards** in the sideboard (optional but standard)
- **Up to 4 copies** of any card (except basic lands and **restricted** cards)

## Format Definition

- **Eternal format**: Cards from all Magic sets are legal (except banned cards)
- **Restricted list**: Some of the most powerful cards are restricted to 1 copy per deck
- **Non-rotating**
- Most powerful constructed format
- Check the [banned and restricted list](https://magic.wizards.com/en/banned-restricted-list#vintage-banned)

## Play Rules

- One-on-one games
- Best of one or best of three
- ~20 minutes average game length

## Common Deckbuilding Ratios

- **Lands**: 14–20 (Moxen and mana artifacts reduce land count)
- **Mana curve**: Very low; fast mana and free spells dominate
- **Interaction**: Counterspells and artifact removal are critical

## Metagame Considerations

- Power Nine and other restricted cards define the format
- Reserved List cards are common and expensive
- Primarily played on MTGO and in paper at high-level events
- Combo decks often win on turn 1–2; disruption is essential
