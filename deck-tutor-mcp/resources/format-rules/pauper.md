# Pauper Format Rules

Use this context when recommending cards for Pauper decks.

## Deck Structure

- **60 cards** minimum in the main deck
- **15 cards** in the sideboard (optional but standard)
- **Up to 4 copies** of any card (except basic lands)

## Format Definition

- **Commons only**: All cards must have been printed at least once at common rarity in a Magic set or product
- If a common version of a card was ever released in paper or MTGO, any printing of that card is legal
- Includes lands printed with common set symbol or L rarity code
- Check the [banned list](https://magic.wizards.com/en/banned-restricted-list#Pauper-banned)

## Play Rules

- One-on-one games
- Best of one or best of three
- ~20 minutes average game length

## Common Deckbuilding Ratios

- **Lands**: 22–26
- **Mana curve**: Often lower due to efficient common removal and threats
- **Interaction**: Key removal and counters are commons

## Metagame Considerations

- Accessible and budget-friendly
- Powerful card combinations despite commons-only restriction
- Diverse strategies: aggro, control, combo, tribal
- Popular on MTGO and in paper
