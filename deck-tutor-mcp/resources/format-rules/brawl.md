# Brawl Format Rules

Use this context when recommending cards for Brawl decks.

## Deck Structure

- **60 cards** total (59 + 1 commander)
- **1 commander** (legendary creature or planeswalker)
- **Singleton**: No more than one copy of any card (except basic lands)
- **No sideboard**

## Format Definition

- Commander-style format with a **60-card deck**
- Uses **Standard-legal sets** only (rotates with Standard)
- Primarily played on **MTG Arena** (1v1)
- Check the [banned list](https://magic.wizards.com/en/banned-restricted-list#Brawl-banned)

## Commander Rules

- Commander must be a **legendary creature** or **planeswalker**
- Commander determines **color identity**: all cards must fit within the commander's colors
- Commander starts in the command zone and can be cast from there
- If the commander would go to graveyard or exile, it may return to the command zone (cost increases by {2} per previous cast)

## Play Rules

- One-on-one games
- Best of one (with free mulligan on Arena)
- **Starting life total: 25**

## Common Deckbuilding Ratios

- **Lands**: ~24–28 (adjust for ramp and curve)
- **Ramp**: 6–10
- **Card draw**: 6–10
- **Interaction**: 4–8

## Metagame Considerations

- More accessible than Commander due to smaller card pool
- Standard rotation means cards change every year
- Arena-focused; paper Brawl is less common
