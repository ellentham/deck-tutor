# Legacy Format Rules

Use this context when recommending cards for Legacy decks.

## Deck Structure

- **60 cards** minimum in the main deck
- **15 cards** in the sideboard (optional but standard)
- **Up to 4 copies** of any card (except basic lands)

## Format Definition

- **Eternal format**: Cards from all Magic sets are legal (except banned cards)
- **Non-rotating**
- Most powerful constructed format besides Vintage
- Check the [banned list](https://magic.wizards.com/en/banned-restricted-list#legacy-banned)

## Play Rules

- One-on-one games
- Best of one or best of three
- ~20 minutes average game length

## Common Deckbuilding Ratios

- **Lands**: 18–24 (many decks use fetchlands and duals)
- **Mana curve**: Highly varied; fast mana and efficient spells common
- **Interaction**: Force of Will, Daze, and removal are staples

## Metagame Considerations

- Very diverse meta: combo, control, tempo, midrange
- Reserved List cards (e.g., dual lands) can be expensive
- Popular on MTGO where card availability differs
- Fast combo decks and efficient answers define the format
