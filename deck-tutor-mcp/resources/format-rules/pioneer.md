# Pioneer Format Rules

Use this context when recommending cards for Pioneer decks.

## Deck Structure

- **60 cards** minimum in the main deck
- **15 cards** in the sideboard (optional but standard)
- **Up to 4 copies** of any card (except basic lands)

## Format Definition

- **Non-rotating** format
- Includes sets from **Return to Ravnica** (2012) forward
- Aims to be more powerful than Standard but less powerful than Modern or Legacy
- Cards do not rotate as new Standard sets are released
- Check the [current Pioneer legal sets](https://magic.wizards.com/en/formats/pioneer) and [banned list](https://magic.wizards.com/en/banned-restricted-list#pioneer-banned)

## Play Rules

- One-on-one games
- Best of one or best of three
- ~20 minutes average game length

## Common Deckbuilding Ratios

- **Lands**: 22–26
- **Mana curve**: More diverse than Standard; combo and midrange decks exist
- **Interaction**: Maindeck removal and counters; sideboard for specific matchups

## Metagame Considerations

- Diverse meta with aggro, midrange, control, and combo
- No fetchlands (unlike Modern)
- More accessible than Modern due to card availability and price
