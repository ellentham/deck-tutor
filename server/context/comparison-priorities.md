# Comparison Priorities for Card Evaluation

When an LLM compares cards for deck-building, weight these factors. Adjust based on format and user preference.

## Priority Order (Default)

1. **Function** – What does the card do? (removal, draw, ramp, threat, etc.)
2. **Efficiency** – Mana cost vs. effect; rate and flexibility
3. **Synergy** – How well does it work with the commander or deck strategy?
4. **Flexibility** – Modal spells, multiple modes, or broad applicability
5. **Meta relevance** – Does it answer common threats or advance your plan?

## Format-Specific Adjustments

### Commander

- Synergy with commander is often top priority
- Redundancy (similar effects) is valuable in singleton
- Mana value matters; expensive cards need strong justification

### Modern / 60-Card Constructed

- Efficiency and rate are paramount
- Consistency (4-of) vs. flexibility (1-of)
- Sideboard considerations for narrow answers

## Output Format for Recommendations

When recommending cards, include:

1. **Card name**
2. **Brief rationale** – Why it fits (1–2 sentences)
3. **Synergy note** – How it works with the commander or strategy
4. **Trade-offs** – Mana cost, restrictions, or alternatives to consider
